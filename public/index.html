FAQs. A few FAQs of the project

Where do you obtain the news articles?
We obtain current articles from the New York Times and the Guardian, and obtain a random sample of articles from the last 24 hours using the Alchemy DataNews API. It in turn sources from various news sites and blogs.
How to you determine the subjects to the news articles?
We use the Alchemy concept tagging API and choose the results with the highest relevant scores.
How often are the site's results updated?
Twitter results are constantly streaming and news results are updated every 4 hours due to API limits.
